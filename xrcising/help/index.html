Frequently Asked Questions
What VR headsets are supported?
xrcising runs in your browser and supports any WebXR-compatible headset, including Meta Quest 2 & 3, HTC Vive, and Valve Index. You can also play in desktop mode without a headset.
Do I need to create an account to play?
You can try songs without an account, but creating a free account lets you save your scores, track progress, and appear on the global leaderboard.
How is my score calculated?
Your score is based on hit accuracy (how close your movements match the beat), your current combo multiplier, and a difficulty bonus. Perfect hits on harder difficulties earn significantly more points.
Can I play without a VR headset?
Yes! Desktop mode lets you preview the scene and interact using your mouse and keyboard. Hand tracking via webcam is also supported on compatible browsers.
How do I report a bug or give feedback?
This is an NYU VR Course project. Please reach out to the development team through your course channel or submit an issue on the project repository.
